User Account Dashboard Redesign - Project Report
Executive Summary
This comprehensive redesign transforms the Artisio user dashboard from a cluttered, unprofessional interface into a sophisticated, user-centric experience that reflects the brand's artisanal craftsmanship. The new design prioritizes visual hierarchy, intuitive navigation, premium aesthetics, and delightful micro-interactions.

Current State Analysis
Critical Issues Identified
1. Visual Hierarchy Problems:

Competing focal points with no clear primary action
Statistics cards lack visual breathing room
Typography hierarchy is weak and inconsistent
Gold accent color overused, diminishing its impact

2. Layout & Spacing Issues:

Header section feels cramped (insufficient padding)
Statistics cards have inconsistent spacing (gaps appear random)
Quick actions section lacks visual distinction from content above
Recent orders section has poor visual separation
Footer content is cluttered and hard to scan

3. User Experience Flaws:

Navigation tabs use inconsistent badge styling
"Discover New Products" CTA feels forced and poorly positioned
Loyalty points notification is visually disconnected
No clear visual progression through the page
Missing personalization depth
Poor mobile responsiveness indicators

4. Branding & Aesthetics:

Design doesn't reflect "artisanal" brand values
Colors feel dated (dark teal header lacks sophistication)
No use of imagery to reinforce brand story
Iconography is generic and flat
Missing premium feel expected from artisan marketplace


Redesign Strategy
Design Principles

Crafted Elegance: Every element should feel handcrafted and intentional
Breathing Space: Generous whitespace reflects premium positioning
Subtle Motion: Animations enhance without distracting
Intuitive Flow: Natural eye movement from hero to actions to content
Personal Connection: Design should feel personal to Sarah
